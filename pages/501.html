Bulab 501

Curing Agent for Flamebloc GS 500 and Flamebloc GS 502
  • Reduces the effect of fire, heat & smoke.
  • Can be dipped or sprayed onto wood.
  • Contains no petroleum derivatives.
  • Passes class “A” flame retardant testing.
 Bulab 501 is a curing agent for Flamebloc GS 500 and Flamebloc GS 502 products. It is blended one part Bulab 501 to 4 parts Flamebloc GS 500 or 502 by volume. After blending, a 5-minute induction period is suggested before the coating is ready for application. After blending with Bulab 501, the product with curing agent can be stirred or blended with a mixer and paddle blade at slow speed avoiding cavitation and air entrapment, mixed for 3-5minutes and then the cured product is ready for application.
TYPICAL CHARACTERISTICS
Appearance Clear to slightly cloudy liquid
Density @25°C (77°F) 1.18 – 1.23 g/ml
Approximate 830 ml
volume in Kilogram

APPLICATION
Application of Bulab 501 when properly combined with Flamebloc GS 500 or 502 is easily accomplished with brush, roller or spray, depending upon which product is used. Since these products are water- soluble, cleanup is accomplished with soap and water wash.
PACKAGING & HANDLING
Bulab 501 is a liquid packaged in drums, intermediate bulk containers and in bulk. Refer to the Material Safety Data Sheet for suitable materials of construction for handling and storing this product.

Improper handling of this product can be injurious to workers. Observe all safety precautions shown on the label and in the Material Safety Data Sheet.
